What do we have to do?

Developers & Designers

  1. More creativity, less copy and paste
  2. Push yourselves outside of your comfort zone
  3. Learn about each other's work
  4. Read the WordPress codex, daily!
  5. Colaborate

Clients

  1. Ask questions often
  2. Talk through your ideas with the team. Listen to them!
